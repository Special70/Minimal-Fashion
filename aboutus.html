About Us

At Minimal, we believe simplicity is powerful. We offer high-quality, stylish essentials and versatile fashion pieces for both men and women. Our focus is on creating timeless clothing and accessories that combine clean, minimal designs with modern style.

We’re committed to quality, simplicity, and functionality, providing a smooth shopping experience that aligns with a minimalist lifestyle. From classic staples to trendy, youthful pieces, everything we offer is made to elevate your everyday wardrobe with ease and style.

Our Story

Minimal was founded by Mikhaiah Lim, a fashion enthusiast passionate about simplicity. Mikhaiah believed that less is more and wanted to create a brand reflecting this philosophy. The goal was to offer high-quality, timeless pieces that seamlessly fit into everyday life.

Minimal is known for its commitment to timeless design and quality craftsmanship. Each piece is designed to last, blending style with practicality. We provide versatile clothing and accessories for both men and women, proving that less is truly more.
